About Me

I'm Neil Mcnaught, and I'm a Junior Web Developer. I'm from Liverpool, United Kingdom and I love all things tech! Since I was a young child, I've always been the person friends seem to come to when they have a problem - and I love it.

I first started out by building very simple WordPress websites, but I wouldn't call that Web Development. I was simply installing Plugins, drag and dropping content. Now a days, I like to build things from scratch, and I especially love Python with the Flask Framework.

When I'm not at my Mac coding I like to spend my spare time being active. I enjoy visiting the gym (When Coronavirus isn't resticting me), spending time with friends and travelling the world. I have recently visited Amsterdam, and of course Turkey.
